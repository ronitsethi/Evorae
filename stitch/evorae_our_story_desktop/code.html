The Art of

Evorae is more than a brand; it is a philosophy of living beautifully. We curate pieces that bridge the gap between ancient heritage and the modern silhouette.

auto_awesome

"Evorae" represents the whisper of an evolving morning — a promise of radiance that begins from within.

Derived from the Latin roots of 'Era' and 'Aurora', our name signifies a new dawn in artisanal fashion. It reflects our belief that luxury should not be loud; it should be felt in the softness of hand-loomed silk and seen in the intentional asymmetry of a hand-set stone.

"Heritage is not what we leave behind, but what we carry forward."

The Origin

A Dialogue Across
Generations

Evorae was born in a sun-drenched atelier in Jaipur, sparked by a conversation between a mother, a lifelong textile historian, and her daughter, a modern designer.

They sought to reconcile the timeless techniques of their ancestors with the fast-paced, global lifestyle of the contemporary woman. Every stitch in our collection is a tribute to this lineage—a bridge between the heritage of the past and the ambition of the future.
