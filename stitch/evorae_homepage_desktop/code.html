The Curator's Philosophy

Our Process

Artistry in every Layer

01

Conscious Sourcing

We partner with ethical mills in Rajasthan that prioritize water conservation and organic fibers.

02

Handblock Printing

Each motif is carved into teak wood and pressed onto fabric by hand, creating subtle, beautiful imperfections.

03

Sun-Drying

Our fabrics are cured in the open desert air, allowing the natural pigments to set with the warmth of the sun.

What makes an Evorae piece?

  • Small Batch Only

    We produce less than 50 units per design to ensure zero waste and true exclusivity.

  • Traceable Origins

    Every item comes with a 'Heritage Passport' detailing the village and artisan who crafted it.

  • Heirloom Quality

    Reinforced seams and high-grade organic threads mean our pieces are made to be passed down.
